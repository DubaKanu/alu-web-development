Healthcare Overview

Healthcare is the maintenance or improvement of health via the prevention, diagnosis, treatment, and recovery of diseases, injuries, and other physical and mental impairments. It involves a range of services provided by various professionals such as doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and more.

Importance of Healthcare

Access to quality healthcare is crucial for improving life expectancy and the overall well-being of individuals and communities. It helps in early detection of diseases, ensures timely treatment, and promotes healthy living practices.

Healthcare Services

Healthcare services include primary care, specialized treatments, emergency services, mental health care, and preventative measures such as vaccinations and screenings.

Healthcare is a fundamental human right and is essential for sustainable development.
